Why bluehawk

The best companies are defined not by their products or services or client lists but by their culture. We have worked to create an employee culture that is based on respect, inclusivity, and empowerment. Time after time our employees tell us that the best thing about Bluehawk is not the work or the clients or even the outstanding benefits, but the culture. Join us and find out what we are all about!

Working With Us

We provide a comprehensive benefits package that gives you security, flexibility, support, and personal choice. We offer benefits that go beyond a paycheck – an opportunity that enables you to build a more enriching and rewarding career. Our benefits include:

 

100% paid gold-level medical, dental, short term disability and vision insurance

401K with immediate vesting: 100% match on first 3%; 50% match on next 2%

Employer-sponsored trainings and certifications

Paid Time Off (PTO) & paid holidays

Employer-sponsored life insurance

Career guidance and support

Competitive salary structure
