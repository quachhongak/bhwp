CASE STUDY

Centralizing Health Care IT Services

The Challenge

Seattle Children’s is ranked among the top 10 children’s hospitals in the nation by U.S. News and World Report, with hospitals, clinics, and research institutes across Washington, Idaho, Montana, and Alaska. Children’s wanted to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of its IT services by creating a centralized service desk to provide IT and application support.

The Solution

Bluehawk worked with the Children’s IT team to design, implement, and rollout a centralized service desk solution to support its hospitals, clinics, and affiliates using ITIL. We established service metrics to gauge the effectiveness of the service desk, improved the alignment and quality of IT services to business objectives, increased customer satisfaction, and improved the availability and consistency of IT services.

“The Bluehawk team has played a significant role in helping us design, implement and operate this centralized Service Desk.”

- Christopher Wisnoski

IT Operations Manager, Seattle Children's
