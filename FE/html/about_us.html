Our Story

Bluehawk was founded on a simple proposition: attract the highest caliber of talent, listen closely for customer needs, and let the magic happen.

Since then, we have earned a reputation as one of the best workplaces in the Pacific Northwest and become a key partner to many of the world's most recognizable brands. Our success, however, has never swayed us from our original goals of being a trusted partner to our clients and a great place to work for our employees.
