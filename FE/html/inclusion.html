Corporate Responsibility

Inclusion

We are a women-owned business with a strong commitment to diversity and inclusion in our workforce, reflecting the diversity of the geographies where we work. We are passionate about creating a positive, rewarding environment for our customers and employees.

Social Responsibility

True to our values of “genuine people” and “shared success,” we understand that we only win if the communities we serve win as well. We take seriously our responsibility to provide a fair, equitable, and just workplace and to work toward those some outcomes in our local, national, and global communities.

Sustainability

As a major employer in our region, we understand that we have a key role to play in reducing consumption and adopting sustainable business practices. We regularly review our practices and our carbon footprint to ensure we are minimizing harm to our environment.

Data Protection

Around the globe and among most major corporations, the bar is being raised for how companies handle and use data. As partners with some of the world’s greatest bands, we take our obligation to protect and responsibly use data seriously.

Privacy

Consumers are growing increasingly savvy about how companies handle and use their data and are taking power back into their own hands to protect their privacy. We wholeheartedly support this movement and follow the strictest standards available when it comes to handling personal and confidential information.
